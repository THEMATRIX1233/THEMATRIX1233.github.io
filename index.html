Disconnected Hours: Finding Refuge,

in Code During Challenging Times

BY Diego Avila Siguenza Grupo 141 TI

From a young age, I’ve always been fascinated by programming. I spent countless hours in cyber cafés, playing games and exploring what the digital world had to offer. My curiosity and desire to understand how games worked led me to experiment with computers. Everything seemed simple, but there was something captivating about solving problems that drew me in. It was then that my mother decided to enroll me in a computer course. There, I met a professor who became my mentor. His passion for programming and ability to explain complex concepts simply inspired me to dive deeper into this intriguing field.

Although I was never the best in class, I always tried my hardest. I remember the nights I stayed up late trying to grasp a new concept or solve a programming challenge. During high school, I discovered structured programming and realized I had a natural talent for it. What seemed like a complicated puzzle to others came relatively easy to me. This realization motivated me to explore the subject further, leading me to delve into various programming languages and tools.

I love programming because it excites me. Each line of code presents a little challenge that I enjoy tackling, and every error I encounter becomes a valuable lesson. I chose web development because, in addition to solving problems, I’m passionate about design. Creating attractive and functional interfaces is something I truly enjoy. The technologies used in web development are fascinating, and there’s always something new to learn, which keeps me motivated and evolving constantly.

Programming has become my refuge, an escape from the problems at home. When I feel overwhelmed or stressed, immersing myself in code allows me to disconnect from difficulties and find a sense of achievement. , while I sometimes feel frustrated facing obstacles, I’ve learned that each challenge is an opportunity for growth. Programming and exercise have become my outlets, enabling me to forget worries and enjoy moments of satisfaction and creativity.
